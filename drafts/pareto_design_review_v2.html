Pareto chart redesign — v2 (use-case x-axis)

All three designs share x = use case (ordered by lift achieved). Choose which best communicates which models drove improvement on which classes.

Option A
Slopegraph — Ensemble (n=5) vs. Hikvision OEM

Two literal lines — the final ensemble vs. the OEM baseline — with sign-aware delta bars at every use case. Green bars mean we beat OEM, red means we still trail. The model name routed to each class sits below the tick.

Option B
Dumbbell — n=1 baseline → n=5 final, colored by driver model

Each use case gets a vertical dumbbell: the gray dot is what one model alone could do; the dark dot is the final ensemble. The connecting bar is colored by the model whose addition delivered the lift, with OEM shown as a green diamond for reference.

Option C
Layered wave — all 5 ensemble lines + OEM

Every step of the ensemble (n=1 to n=5) is plotted on top of the OEM baseline. Lines fade from light to dark blue as more models are added; the n=5 line and the OEM line are emphasized so the final "us vs. them" comparison stays visible.
