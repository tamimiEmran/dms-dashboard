Interpretation
Best under-25 MB candidate

mediapipe + yolo_soham + yolov10s_coco is the clean size-target candidate: 23 MB, 28.2 GFLOPs/frame, and 71.1% mean balanced accuracy.

Where the accuracy goes

Distracted, eating, fatigue, and seatbelt remain unchanged versus v2 in this substitution. Phone usage drops from 83.3% to 63.6% balanced accuracy.

Metric consistency

OEM, current v2, and compact v2 are all shown as balanced accuracy. The older 81.x% v2 number was raw mean accuracy and is intentionally not used for this comparison.
