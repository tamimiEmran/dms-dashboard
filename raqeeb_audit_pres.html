Fair subset methodology: Each model only covers a subset of the 6 OEM classes (e.g., drowsiness models output "drowsy" or "alert", not "phone_usage"). The fair subset column evaluates only on videos whose ground truth matches classes the model can produce. For drowsiness models: fatigue + safe_driving (161 of 345 videos). For distraction models: the StateFarm 10-class scheme maps to 4 OEM classes (distracted_driving, phone_usage, drinking_eating, safe_driving) via label remapping — e.g., c1–c4 → phone_usage, c5/c7/c8/c9 → distracted_driving. Videos labeled fatigue or seatbelt are excluded since no StateFarm class maps to them.
