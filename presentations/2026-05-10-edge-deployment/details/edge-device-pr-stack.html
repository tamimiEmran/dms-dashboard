01 · Camera
MaixCam-2 (Sipeed)
Smart camera with on-board AX630C NPU. Captures up to 4K @ 30 fps over a 4-lane MIPI CSI sensor and runs YOLO11n inference at the device, so the OBU sees pre-filtered events rather than raw video.

03 · SoC / compute
TZTEK TADC-J6M (Horizon Journey 6M)
The chip-level view of the AI compute. Six Cortex-A78AE application cores feed a 128 DL-TOPS BPU (Bayes architecture); a separate 4× R52 lockstep MCU and dual-ISP front-end handle safety and vision pre-processing.
