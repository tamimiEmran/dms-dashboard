CAN-bus

CAN-bus corroboration for DMS events

Pair the DMS camera view with vehicle turn-signal data so risky lane-change events are checked from both driver action and vehicle state.

Camera + vehicle signal
Use CAN-bus turn indicators to verify what the DMS camera sees.

The camera shows whether the driver checked before a maneuver. The CAN-bus signal shows whether the vehicle indicator was active in the same window. Together they make the event easier to review than either source alone.
