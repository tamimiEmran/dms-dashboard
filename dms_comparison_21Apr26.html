6. Known Errors

Custom Model — Current Error Modes

Observed misclassifications that require label refinement or threshold tuning

Drinking vs. Phone: hand-to-face posture triggers "DRINKING 0.53" when the driver is actually using a phone. Stacked alerts fire simultaneously (YAWNING, DISTRACTED, STOP DRINKING!).
Drinking + Smoking overlap: YOLO fires both "DRINKING 0.72" and "SMOKING 0.47" on the same frame alongside EYES CLOSED and DISTRACTED — multi-label arbitration needed.
Label refinement: phone is mislabeled as DRINKING — retrain YOLO head with clean phone-only class.
Alert arbitration: deduplicate overlapping hand-near-face triggers (phone / drinking / smoking).
