Approach

Encoder selection

Three encoders on the same source clips. SVT-AV1 picked on size-at-quality.

Source: H.264 · 1920×1080 · 25 fps
Target: SVT-AV1 · 854×480 · 2 fps · no audio
Constraint: archive + inference, same footage
Ruled out
Apple VideoToolbox
Hardware H.265
Hardware HEVC on Apple Silicon. Fast, low CPU. Rate-distortion degrades at the bitrates we wanted; quality falls off before hitting the target size.
Not picked
libx265
Software H.265
Software HEVC. Competitive on quality, but needs more bits than AV1 at matched cabin fidelity on this footage.
Selected
SVT-AV1
Software AV1
Lowest bitrate at the quality threshold used. Cabin detail holds at ~4% of the source bitrate; background texture softens.

Pipeline

Raw H.264 · 1080p · 25 fps
→
Downscale to 854×480
→
Resample to 2 fps
→
Drop audio
→
SVT-AV1 encode
Note on 2 fps. The DMS model samples frames sparsely, so every decision window is kept. Audio carries no signal we use and is dropped. The resulting footprint is small enough to archive the whole fleet.
